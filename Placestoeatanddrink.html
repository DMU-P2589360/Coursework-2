Steak and Company

We will turn over a new leaf as Steak and Company, along with a brand-new menu of contemporary, vibrant dishes across all our branches. *Hot stones and Halal are discontinued from 24th April 2022. At Steak & Co. quality runs through our veins, and we are so proud of everything we do. We cater to the most dedicated carnivore with the Steak side of our menu and those who are looking for something different in our & Co. side of the menu. Serving only the finest steak, which has been carefully selected and prepared by our master butchers and cooked to perfection. The steaks are first caramelized on our chargrill to seal in the flavour then you choose an infused butter, a salt or shake and a sauce as a final accompaniment. Our other three restaurants are located in London’s West End with the flagship branch in Leicester Square, another in the vibrant Piccadilly Circus, and a local neighbourhood favourite in Gloucester Road.
